04.

MIV

MIV - Visual Identity Manual - is a document prepared after the approval of a brand. Within the Manual of Visual Guidelines, known as MIV, are recorded the essential guidelines, technical specifications and regulations that contribute to consistency in the graphic compositions and promotion tactics adopted by the company.

When the organisation decides on its logo and name, it needs a guide to advise on the proper application of the corporate identity.

This is one of the reasons why an IVM becomes indispensable. It also provides standardisation of applications in various contexts, such as facades, establishments, printed materials, among others.

The visual identity manual is moulded according to the requirements of the company or professional, and can vary in complexity or simplicity according to these specificities.

In this way, it is simplified to ensure the success of campaigns and promote brand recognition among a wider audience, following a consistent standard - MIV.
